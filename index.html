Shoulder to Shoulder in the Backcountry.

Stepping out of the NZDF leaves a gap. The routine, the shared purpose, the people standing next to you—it all changes overnight. We get it, because we've lived it.

SSAANZ runs monthly events across Aotearoa designed specifically for you and your whānau. We focus on three things that matter: building mental and physical resilience, sharing vital wilderness skills, and keeping our community tight.

Whether you're summiting a peak or just needing a quiet sit by the fire with people who understand, there’s a place for you here. The objective is simple: take the skills you sharpen with us, and use them to ground yourself in your everyday civilian life.
